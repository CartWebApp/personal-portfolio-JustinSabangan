Research / Synthesis — Overview

Project 1 Research / Synthesis Essay

A research paper where I had to synthesize multiple sources to write a claim and analysis on the topic of zoo enrichment.

Problems & Solutions
  • The Problem: I struggled with keeping track of my sources and their information.
  • The Solution: I wrote notes and tried to simplify where I found my information.
  • The Problem: Finding sources and if they had good information or not was really difficult because I would waste a lot of time or just not find anything at all regarding my specific topic.
  • The Solution: I would skim through the sources to see if there was valid information, and if there was not, I would use different source finders and AI to find a source.
  • The Problem: Getting an interview was a difficult process because many zoos did not respond back.
  • The Solution: I emailed different zoos and kept trying to find a zoo that would actually reply back. Eventually, one zoo did reply to my questions and gave me helpful information to use.
Concepts learned
  • Synthesis: I learned a lot of information about the topic that I was assigned to and put all of those thoughts together to support my claim.
  • Research: During the research process, I learned how to find information that was valuable to what I was writing about and figure out when to stop reading invaluable sources.
Habits of Mind
  • Persistence: Gathering information, putting all my thoughts together, and actually writing the essay took a lot of time, and I would be a bit burnt out of doing it; however, I told myself to keep going and just let the writing flow through me.
  • Communication: I had to communicate with a zoo and be mindful of their time because they are taking the time out of their day to answer my questions.
Argumentative / Synthesis — Overview

Project 2 Argumentative / Synthesis Essay

An argumentative essay where I had to synthesize 3 sources about the attention economy.

Problems & Solutions
  • Argumentative Claim: Using the information from the limited 3 sources, I wrote an argumentative claim about how platforms have the power in the attention economy.
  • Writing Under Pressure: This essay was a timed assignment, meaning that I had only 3 hours to write all of my body paragraphs and analysis. Time is of the essence!
Concepts learned
  • CSS Styling: I had a basic knowledge of CSS selectors and only knew a few. In this project, my understanding increased and the usage of 42+ selectors helped my knowledge overall.
  • Graphic Design: For my main aesthetic, I had to design graphics for my headers based on a video game aesthetic that I found to be cool to use.
Habits of Mind
  • Creativity and Innovation: Designing graphics and how I would execute my design expanded my creativity.
  • Application of Past Knowledge: Previously, I had done many CSS exercises to help me with layouts, so I applied that knowledge to help change the layout of my design.
